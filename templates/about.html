About RailBook

India's leading railway booking platform, making travel accessible and convenient for millions of passengers since 2010.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, RailBook started with a simple mission: to make train travel booking effortless and accessible to everyone across India.

What began as a small startup in Mumbai has now grown into one of India's most trusted railway booking platforms, serving over 5 million customers annually.

Our journey has been fueled by innovation, customer-centric approach, and a passion for simplifying travel. We continuously evolve our platform to meet the changing needs of modern travelers.

Our Mission

To make train travel booking seamless, accessible, and convenient for every Indian, leveraging technology to enhance the travel experience.

To become India's most trusted travel platform, revolutionizing how people plan, book, and experience train journeys across the country.
